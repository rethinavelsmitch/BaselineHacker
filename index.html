How this estimate works

Your Baseline Score is a composite metric (0–100) derived from blood biomarkers, lifestyle data, and health indicators. Each biomarker is weighted by clinical significance and your personal variance.

When you toggle interventions:

  • Each action maps to specific biomarkers it can improve.
  • Individual impacts are based on published clinical averages.
  • Combined effects use a diminishing-returns model — each additional action contributes slightly less than in isolation.
  • A hard cap of +12.0 points prevents unrealistic additive inflation.
  • Overlapping biomarker effects are de-duplicated (e.g., sleep + yoga both affect Cortisol).

The confidence level decreases as more actions are combined, reflecting real-world uncertainty in multi-variable predictions.

Score band thresholds
  • 0–39 — Compromised
  • 40–54 — Struggling
  • 55–69 — Stable
  • 70–84 — Healthy
  • 85–100 — Elite
Data sources & limitations

Estimates rely on population-level clinical data. Individual responses may vary based on genetics, adherence, baseline health, and other factors not captured in this model.

This is a directional planning tool, not a medical prediction. Always consult your physician before making health changes.
